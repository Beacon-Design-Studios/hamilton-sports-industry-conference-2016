What is it?

An opportunity for students to meet professionals in the sports industry!

Hamilton Sports Industry Conference invites you to our 2016 Conference! Located in Celebration Hall at McMaster University, #HSIC2016 will be a full day of networking, speeches, and panel discussions that will provide students, young proffesionals, and seasoned industry experts an opportunity to learn and gather in the name of all things mighty, sports.

  • Why you should come out
  • Who should come out

If you're trying to enter the sports industry because you're passionate about sports, this is a great event to learn from successful people in the field!

  • Prominent Speakers
  • Food
  • Networking
  • Keynotes & tips!

If you love athletics and recreation and are thinking of pursuing a meaningful career in the industry whether it be as a financial manager, consultant, rehabilitation expert, medical practitioner or any of the professions you can follow in this industry, this is an opportunity you don't want to miss out on!
